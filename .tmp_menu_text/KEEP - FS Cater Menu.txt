Production Fee:
Our 30% production fee is essential for ensuring that every event runs smoothly while
maintaining the high quality of service and food that you expect from us. This fee covers
several important costs of executing your event, including team commissions & gratuity,
event logistics, supplies, and other operational costs.
Labor:
Labor is determined on a per event basis depending on the size and complexity of your
unique event. This expense reflects the extensive preparation that goes into ensuring
your event's success well before and after the actual service time. Keep in mind that
venues often have specific requirements for caterers. Your quote may be adjusted to
include labor requirements from your venue.
Deposit:
To secure your event date, a 25% deposit is required upon contract signing. Since we only
have a limited number of dates available, the deposit is non-refundable. This ensures that
we can dedicate our time and resources to making your event unforgettable.
Payment Schedule:
Final payment and guest count is due 14 days prior to the event. Should there be any last
minute additions to the menu, guest count, or services provided, we will send you an
updated invoice the following business day. If capable, we are happy to accommodate
your last-minute requests!
